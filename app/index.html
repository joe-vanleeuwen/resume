About

My interest in computer programming began early. My brother dabbled in programming and I was quite amazed when he drew a circle with code. As a senior in high school I took my first programming course and enjoyed every minute, though I pursued a Graphic Design degree in college. I graduated having a Humanities degree under my belt and rather unsure of what to do next.

While doing manufacturing work at BMW as a Production Associate, I began to reignite my interest in programming by taking lessons in Javascript from Codeacademy. When I heard about The Iron Yard Academy's Front End Engineering course, I took the plunge and have no regrets. Now I do freelance web development work and enjoy some serious Javascript coding for fun.
